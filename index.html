Scriptura (Sola)

Your companion for inductive Bible study – free and grounded

Try Free Version on Poe Try Pro Version on ChatGPT+

What Scriptura (Sola) Helps You Do

  • Follow inductive study steps: Observation → Interpretation → Application
  • Ask guided questions and avoid theological bias
  • Study by verse, chapter, book, or topic
  • Practice charting, context tracing, and character study
  • Use Strong’s concordance (ChatGPT+ only)

What It Won’t Do

  • Impose doctrinal interpretations or replace personal study
  • Skip inductive process to give quick answers
  • Assume your life context — it asks you to reflect instead

Poe Version (Free)

  • No account required (limited messages per day)
  • All inductive logic built-in
  • Accessible via mobile and web
  • Faster initial setup
  • Cannot export study files

ChatGPT+ Version ($20/mo)

  • Uses full Strong’s Hebrew + Greek files
  • Can generate PDF, Word, and PowerPoint studies
  • Remembers uploads and tracks progress
  • Better formatting and structured sessions
