Scriptura (Sola)

Inductive Bible Study Powered by AI

Try Scriptura on Poe (Free) Try Scriptura on ChatGPT+ (Full)

What Scriptura (Sola) Can Help You Do

  • Observe, interpret, and apply Scripture using the inductive method
  • Look up Strong’s words in Greek and Hebrew
  • Chart verses, identify structure, repetition, and themes
  • Ask genre-based and culturally aware questions
  • Explore characters, concepts, and biblical history

What Scriptura (Sola) Will Not Do

  • Impose theology or denominational interpretation
  • Skip observation steps to jump to application
  • Offer devotional fluff instead of textual clarity

  • No file exports
  • No session memory
  • Embeddable in webpages
