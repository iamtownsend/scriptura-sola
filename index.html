Scriptura (Sola)

AI-Powered Inductive Bible Study Tool

Try Scriptura on Poe (Free) Try Scriptura on ChatGPT+ (Full)

What Scriptura (Sola) Helps You Do

  • Practice inductive Bible study (Observation → Interpretation → Application)
  • Explore repetition, structure, literary context, and historical backdrop
  • Get verse breakdowns and verse charting assistance
  • Ask genre-specific questions and perform basic thematic searches
  • Access Strong’s numbers (ChatGPT+ version only)

What It Won’t Do

  • Force a doctrinal interpretation
  • Skip the text and jump straight to life application
  • Preach or replace your own study or spiritual discernment

Poe Version (Free)

  • Free and accessible with message limits
  • Structured inductive flow
  • Strong’s: limited lookup only
  • No file export or memory
  • Embeddable on websites

ChatGPT+ Version ($20/mo)

  • Full Strong’s dictionary support (Greek + Hebrew)
  • Can export studies as PDF, Word, and PowerPoint
  • Persistent memory for deeper study tracking
  • Accesses uploads and performs file generation
  • Better formatting and response refinement
